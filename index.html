Discover our Europe tours

Adventure awaits, right on your doorstep! With so many cultures and countries just a short flight away, our unique Europe tours offer far more than you could imagine.

From glorious summers spent relaxing on beaches or exploring historic towns, to snowy winters spent on the slopes or sipping hot chocolate by the fire, our adventure holidays in Europe are designed to keep you entertained and  captivated, season by season.

Stretching from the countries of the former USSR to the Atlantic islands of Iceland and Greenland, Europe is one of the planet’s most beautifully diverse regions on the planet. Explore a new culture, delve into ancient ruins, or make new friends on an action-packed adventure: our trips to Europe let you see and experience it all.

Start building your dream trip, with our expert local guides, and choose by destination or type of activity. Whether you want to travel to  Italy, Spain, Croatia, Portugal or Greece, each country or region has its own unique offering, and is sure to be an incredible adventure.

Five amazing experiences in Europe

Exploring the Amalfi Coast
We’ll help you understand one of Italy’s most quintessential coasts, famed for its dreamily steep cliffs, colourful, stylish seaside towns, and endless panoramas.

Hiking the Alps
Our range of European tours in the Alps lets walkers of all ages and fitness levels experience the stark, dramatic landscapes of this majestic region.

Discovering Greenland
Rugged and unspoilt, our Greenland tours let you experience the dizzying vistas and unforgettable wildlife of one of Earth’s last true wildernesses.

Madeira
Lush and striking, Madeira has some of the most otherworldly sights in Europe: subtropical laurel forests, black-sanded beaches, and all sorts of unique flora and fauna.

Our guides will introduce you to this one-of-a-kind region – it’s passionate, independent culture, classic picture-perfect views, and deep history stretching back to antiquity.

FAQs on Europe Tours

Can I travel to Europe from the UK after Brexit?

Yes, you can travel to Europe from the UK after Brexit, but there is a chance you’ll need to get a visa, extra travel documents, and you will almost certainly need travel insurance that covers healthcare.

To learn more about what the requirements are for your chosen destination, view the government’s travel advice or contact our team.

How much does it cost to tour Europe?

The cost of your tour around Europe depends on several factors, including the length of your trip, the locations you choose to visit, activities, and whether you book including or excluding flights.

Exodus caters for every kind of traveller, so to understand how much your chosen adventure holiday to Europe might cost, view our trips and use the search filters to tailor your selection to your tour needs.
When is the best time to tour Europe?

The answer to when the best time is to tour Europe depends on your personal preferences! Indeed, every corner of the continent is worth visiting through all four seasons, from snowy and stark winter through to bright and welcoming summer.

If you are looking to visit well-travelled locations without crowds, the ‘shoulder seasons’ of April to mid-June and September to October have fewer crowds and comfortable temperatures. Of course, many of our European tours visit lesser-travelled corners of the continent, so feel free to book during your favourite season, and if you want to know the best time to tour your chosen destination, please get in touch.
What are the best places to visit in Europe?

Europe is enormously varied, offering unforgettable experiences for travellers of all persuasions, so deciding the best places to visit comes down to what you enjoy doing most.

Whether you love hiking, city breaks, mountains, rolling countryside, coastlines, forests, or anything in-between, get inspired via our Expert Insights hub of guides and articles.
What is the best way to tour Europe for the first time?

If you’re touring Europe for the first time, you may be wondering how to make the most out of your experience.

Travelling with a group of like-minded travellers  can be a great choice for first-timers, given your flights, train bookings, transfers, accommodations, meals, and activities will be all taken care of. Guided by experts, you can spend all your time enjoying your destination.

First time on an adventure holiday? Make sure to pay attention to our tour listings when booking, including the activity level. Checking out our guides – such as top tips for beginner trekkers – can help you prepare too.
What types of activities can I do on my trip to Europe?

We offer a wide range of activities for our Europe tour groups. You can choose to take a trip focused on a single activity, like hiking, or select a mixed-activity holiday packed with all sorts of things to do – from the laid-back to the pulse-raising.

Walking, cycling, trekking, wildlife watching, cruises, cultural trips, and sports like climbing, kayaking, and snorkelling all feature – view our activities to learn more.
Are flights included on your Europe tours?

Flight can be included in your trip as we offer both land-only and flight-inclusive bookings.

Our Europe trips with flights have a designated group flight per holiday. This is the recommended flight to use with the trip and will be the one the tour leader meets you at when you start your holiday.
Can I take the train to Europe?

Of course, you can. And taking the train is easier than you might think as we continue to expand our train-booking services across our European trips.

Travelling by train on your next European tour will both increase your sense of adventure and lower your carbon impact. If you’re thinking of booking your tickets, just call a member of our Exodus team for routes, prices, and how to book.
What is the duration of your tours in Europe?

Although each trip is different from the next, a lot of our adventures start at eight days, with some reaching over 14 days.

Whether you’re dreaming of getting away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life on a cycling holiday in Europe, or breathing in the crisp, fresh winter air in the stunning surroundings of Lapland, we have all kinds of perfect Europe holidays waiting for you.
Are your Europe tours guided?

Most of our Europe tours feature small, guided groups, which is a great way to explore the places you want to see with like-minded travellers.

We also have self-guided holidays, private adventures, and tours perfect for premium adventurers. No matter what type of experience you’re looking for, we have you covered.
